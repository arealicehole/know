# B2B Print Shops Social Media Success Guide 2025

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive research report analyzes successful B2B print companies on social media in 2025, focusing on Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook strategies. The findings reveal that while many print shops struggle with social media, those who succeed follow specific patterns: educational content mixed with portfolio showcases, consistent posting schedules, and strategic use of video content particularly through Reels.

**Key Findings:**
- B2B companies on Instagram see average engagement rates of 1-5%, with 1.16% being typical
- Instagram generates 22.53 interactions per 1000 followers per post - 20x more than LinkedIn for B2B
- Optimal posting frequency: 2 posts per week on Facebook/Instagram for maximum engagement
- Video content (Reels) outperforms static images by 38% on average
- Carousels achieve highest engagement rate at 1.92% vs 1.74% for single images
- TikTok users expect 1-4 posts daily, though average is 2 videos per week
- 97% of business owners use social media as part of their marketing strategy

### 6.2 Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Posting Too Frequently Without Strategy**
- 19.7% of marketers post multiple times daily
- Leads to lower quality content
- Better to post twice weekly with high quality

❌ **Talking AT Audiences Instead of WITH Them**
- Mono-directional communication fails
- Need to engage in conversations
- Respond to comments and DMs

❌ **Jumping on Every Viral Trend**
- Research trends for brand fit first
- Forced trending content feels inauthentic
- Better to skip than do poorly

❌ **Treating All Platforms Identically**
- Each platform has unique culture
- TikTok ≠ Instagram ≠ Facebook
- Tailor content to platform norms

❌ **Technical Mistakes**
- Broken links damage credibility
- Spelling errors undermine professionalism
- Inappropriate hashtags confuse audience

❌ **Partnering with Wrong Influencers**
- Must match brand values
- Relevant to print/B2B audience
- Better to skip than mismatch

❌ **No Clear Strategy or Goals**
- Posting without purpose
- No tracking or measurement
- Can't optimize what you don't measure

❌ **Tone-Deaf or Insensitive Content**
- Chase Bank's patronizing money tips example
- Burger King's "Women belong in kitchen" backfire
- McDonald's incomplete draft tweet going live
- Always consider how content could be interpreted

❌ **Ignoring Community Management**
- Not responding to comments/DMs
- Missing opportunities to convert inquiries
- Damages relationship building

❌ **Not Tracking ROI**
- 58% of B2B marketers struggle with ROI measurement
- Need UTM parameters and analytics setup
- Can't prove value without data
