## DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Color Palettes (1-2 colors for screen printing)
- **Primary:** Black on khaki/tan/bone/white
- **Secondary:** Black + red accents
- **Arizona colors:** Copper, cardinal red, desert tan
- **Aztec colors:** Jade green, turquoise, gold

### Typography
- Pointed Old English / Placa script (90s Chicano wall style)
- Extra long spikes on letters like B, R, A, Z
- Clean but aggressive

### Cultural Elements
- Aztec glyphs, pyramids, calendar, warriors
- Arizona-specific: saguaro, diamondback, copper star, 602/480
- Lowrider culture: '64 Impala, Daytons, hydraulics
- Religious: Virgen de Guadalupe, rosaries, sacred heart

### Print Specs
- Designed for screen printing (1-2 color max for cost efficiency)
- Works on: tees, hoodies, dickies, flannels, beanies
- Placement options: full chest, left chest pocket hit, back hitter
