- TikTok Business Account (required - personal accounts NOT supported)

- Only business accounts eligible

## Setup Instructions by Platform

### Instagram Setup (Using ManyChat - Recommended)

**Prerequisites:**
1. Instagram Business or Creator account
2. Facebook Page linked to Instagram
3. ManyChat account created

**Step-by-Step Setup:**

**1. Connect Instagram to ManyChat**
   - Log into ManyChat dashboard
   - Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Channels
   - Click "Connect Instagram"
   - Authorize ManyChat to access your Instagram Business account
   - Select the Instagram account to connect
   - Confirm permissions (DM access, comment access, story access)

**2. Create Comment-to-DM Automation**
   - Go to Automation ‚Üí Growth Tools
   - Select "Instagram Comment Trigger"
   - Choose the Instagram post or set to "All Posts"
   - Click "Create New Trigger"

**3. Set Up Trigger Keywords**
   - Add trigger words/phrases (e.g., "INFO", "GUIDE", "DETAILS", "PRICING")
   - Set up multiple variations for each trigger:
     - Example: "info", "INFO", "more info", "send info"
   - Enable case-insensitive matching
   - Optional: Set up emoji triggers (e.g., üî•, üíØ, üôã)

**4. Design the DM Flow**
   - Create initial message (see Templates section below)
   - Add delays between messages (recommended: 2-5 seconds)
   - Include clear call-to-action (book call, download resource, visit link)
   - Add qualification questions if needed:
     - "What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?"
     - "What size is your business? (Solo/Small/Mid/Enterprise)"
     - "When are you looking to get started?"

**5. Set Up Conditions and Logic**
   - Use conditional branching based on responses
   - Tag users based on interest level ("hot-lead", "nurture", "not-interested")
   - Route qualified leads to CRM or calendar booking
   - Set up follow-up sequences for non-responders

**6. Test Your Automation**
   - Post a test Instagram post
   - Comment with your trigger word from another account
   - Verify DM arrives correctly
   - Check message formatting and links
   - Test all branches of conversation flow

**7. Activate and Monitor**
   - Turn automation live
   - Monitor first 24 hours closely
   - Check for any errors or failed DMs
   - Adjust trigger words based on actual comments received

**Estimated Setup Time:** 30-45 minutes for first automation, 15 minutes for subsequent flows

### Instagram Setup (Using Meta Business Suite - Free)

**Prerequisites:**
1. Instagram Business or Creator account
2. Facebook Page linked to Instagram account

**Step-by-Step Setup:**

**1. Access Meta Business Suite**
   - Go to business.facebook.com
   - Log in with Facebook account connected to Instagram
   - Ensure Instagram Business account appears in account list

**2. Navigate to Automation Settings**
   - From main dashboard, click "Inbox" in left sidebar
   - Look for "Automations" in the top menu
   - Click "Automations" to open automation settings

**3. Create Keyword-Based Reply**
   - Select "Add new automation" or similar option
   - Choose "Keyword-based replies" or "Instant replies"
   - Set automation to trigger on Instagram DMs or comments

**4. Configure Trigger Keywords**
   - Enter trigger words (e.g., "INFO", "PRICING", "GUIDE")
   - Note: Native tools have simpler keyword matching
   - Cannot use complex rules or multiple conditions
   - Add multiple keyword variations manually

**5. Write Auto-Reply Message**
   - Compose the DM message to send automatically
   - Keep it concise (character limits may apply)
   - Include clear next step (link, instruction, question)
   - Use personalization tokens if available ({{first_name}})

**6. Set Automation Schedule**
   - Choose when automation is active (24/7 or business hours)
   - Set away messages for off-hours if needed
   - Configure FAQ responses for common questions

**7. Test and Activate**
   - Save automation
   - Test by commenting trigger word from test account
   - Verify DM is received correctly
   - Make adjustments as needed

**Limitations to Note:**
- Cannot create multi-step flows
- No advanced conditional logic
- Limited personalization
- Basic analytics only
- Feature availability varies by region

**Estimated Setup Time:** 15-20 minutes

### Trigger Word Best Practices

**1. Use Multiple Variations**
Set up multiple keywords for same automation:
- Primary: "GUIDE"
- Variations: "guide", "GUIDE ME", "send guide", "download guide"
- Natural: "I want the guide", "can I get the guide"

**2. Test & Optimize**
- Start with 3-5 trigger words per automation
- Monitor which ones get used most
- Remove underperforming triggers
- A/B test different words for same offer

**3. Call Out the Trigger Word in Content**
Make it crystal clear in your post:
- ‚ùå Vague: "Comment if you want this"
- ‚úÖ Clear: "Comment 'GUIDE' to get the free AI toolkit"
- ‚úÖ Better: "Type 'GUIDE' below and I'll DM it to you instantly üëá"

**4. Create Scarcity Around the Trigger**
- "Comment 'TEMPLATE' in the next 24 hours"
- "First 50 people to comment 'DEMO' get a bonus"
- "Limited spots - comment 'CALL' now"

**5. Make It Feel Special**
- "Comment 'VIP' for exclusive access"
- "Insiders: comment 'EARLY' for first look"
- "Comment 'UNLOCK' to get behind-the-scenes"

**6. Avoid Over-Triggered Words**
Don't use extremely common words that might trigger accidentally:
- ‚ùå "YES" (too common in regular comments)
- ‚ùå "THANKS" (people say this naturally)
- ‚ùå "NICE" (generic engagement)
- ‚úÖ "GUIDE" (specific, intentional)
- ‚úÖ "TOOLKIT" (unique, purposeful)

**7. Match Trigger to Content Type**

| Content Type | Good Triggers | Why |
|--------------|---------------|-----|
| Educational Tutorial | "GUIDE", "STEPS", "HOW" | Signals desire to learn |
| Case Study | "RESULTS", "CASE", "EXAMPLE" | Shows proof-seeking |
| Tool Showcase | "TOOL", "LINK", "ACCESS" | Action-oriented |
| Template Share | "TEMPLATE", "DOWNLOAD", "COPY" | Clear deliverable |
| Service Promotion | "PRICING", "DEMO", "CALL" | Sales-ready |

## Message Templates That Convert

### Template Strategy Framework

**Winning DM Structure:**
1. **Hook** (1 sentence) - Acknowledge why they commented
2. **Value** (1-2 sentences) - What they're getting
3. **Deliver** (1 action) - Give them what they asked for
4. **Qualify** (1 question) - Understand their needs
5. **CTA** (1 clear ask) - Next step to become customer

**Character Limits (2025):**
- Instagram: 1,000 characters per message (without buttons), 640 characters (with buttons)
- Facebook Messenger: 2,000 characters recommended
- TikTok: 1,000 characters

**Timing Best Practices:**
- First message: Instant (0 delay)
- Second message: 2-5 second delay
- Third message: 5-10 second delay
- Don't exceed 3-4 messages in initial sequence

---

**Use Case:** Delivering free guide, checklist, or resource
**Trigger Word:** "GUIDE", "TOOLKIT", "CHECKLIST"


**Why It's Better:**

- Conversational tone

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

#### Issue: DMs Not Being Delivered

**Symptoms:**
- Trigger comments happening, but no DMs sent
- Low delivery rate (<90%)

**Possible Causes & Fixes:**

**Cause 1: Hit Rate Limits**
- Fix: Reduce sending volume, enable slow-down mode

**Cause 2: Spam Filters Triggered**
- Fix: Review message content, remove spammy language, add more personalization

**Cause 3: Account Flagged**
- Fix: Stop automation for 48 hours, reduce volume when resuming

**Cause 4: Integration Issue**
- Fix: Disconnect and reconnect Instagram in automation tool

**Cause 5: User Has DMs Restricted**
- Fix: Nothing you can do - some users block DMs from non-followers

#### Issue: Low Response Rate to DMs

**Symptoms:**
- DMs delivered but few people responding
- Open rate is high but response rate <15%

**Possible Causes & Fixes:**

**Cause 1: Message Not Engaging**
- Fix: Rewrite opening message with better hook, ask easier question

**Cause 2: No Clear Next Step**
- Fix: Add specific CTA, make response easy (buttons, multiple choice)

**Cause 3: Too Much Text**
- Fix: Shorten message, break into smaller chunks

**Cause 4: Wrong Timing**
- Fix: Test different send times (though instant is usually best)

**Cause 5: Value Not Clear**
- Fix: Lead with benefit, make value obvious immediately

---

#### Issue: High Unqualified Lead Rate

**Symptoms:**
- Lots of responses but few actually interested in buying
- Sales team complaining about lead quality

**Possible Causes & Fixes:**

**Cause 1: No Qualification in Automation**
- Fix: Add qualifying questions (budget, timeline, company size)

**Cause 2: Attracting Wrong Audience**
- Fix: Refine content targeting, pre-qualify in post (e.g., "This is for businesses doing $X+")

**Cause 3: Offer Misalignment**
- Fix: Make sure lead magnet aligns with paid offer

**Cause 4: Too Broad Targeting**
- Fix: Create more specific content for ideal customer profile

#### Issue: Action Block / Account Restriction

**Symptoms:**
- "We've detected automated behavior on your account"
- Can't send DMs temporarily
- Account activity restricted

**What Triggered It:**
- Too many DMs too fast
- Sudden spike in activity
- Multiple spam reports
- Bot-like behavior detected

**Immediate Actions:**
- Stop ALL automation immediately
- Don't try to circumvent block
- Wait out the restriction (usually 24-72 hours)
- Don't delete anything (looks suspicious)

**After Block Lifts:**
- Resume at 50% previous volume
- Improve message quality (more personalization)
- Ensure using official API tools only
- Gradually increase volume over 2-3 weeks

**Prevention:**
- Warm up accounts gradually
- Use rate limiting
- Mix automation with human activity
- Maintain high-quality messages

---

#### Issue: Integration Not Working (Zapier/CRM)

**Symptoms:**
- Leads not appearing in CRM
- Zapier showing errors
- Data not syncing correctly

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

**Step 1: Check Zap Status**
- Is Zap turned on?
- Are you within task limits?

**Step 2: Review Zap History**
- Look for error messages
- Check which step failed

**Step 3: Test Zap Manually**
- Use "Test" feature in Zapier
- Send test lead through ManyChat

**Step 4: Verify Permissions**
- Reauthorize connections if needed
- Check API keys still valid

**Step 5: Check Field Mapping**
- Ensure all required fields mapped
- Verify data types match

**Step 6: Contact Support**
- ManyChat support for their side
- Zapier support for integration issues
- CRM support for receiving end

---

#### Issue: Low Trigger Comment Rate

**Symptoms:**
- Lots of post engagement (likes, regular comments)
- But few people commenting trigger words
- <5% trigger comment rate

**Possible Causes & Fixes:**

**Cause 1: Trigger Word Not Obvious**
- Fix: Make CTA more prominent, call out trigger word verbally (if video)

**Cause 2: Offer Not Compelling**
- Fix: Improve perceived value, add social proof, create urgency

**Cause 3: Audience Doesn't Understand**
- Fix: Explain what happens when they comment ("Comment GUIDE and I'll DM it to you")

**Cause 4: Wrong Trigger Word**
- Fix: Test different trigger words, use simpler/more intuitive words

**Cause 5: Content Not Engaging Enough**
- Fix: Improve content quality, focus on high-performing content types

---

#### Issue: High Opt-Out Rate

**Symptoms:**
- Many people opting out after first message
- "STOP" responses
- Complaints

**Possible Causes & Fixes:**

**Cause 1: Too Salesy/Pushy**
- Fix: Lead with value, remove aggressive sales language

**Cause 2: Not What They Expected**
- Fix: Align DM content with what post promised

**Cause 3: Too Many Messages**
- Fix: Reduce message frequency, consolidate into fewer messages

**Cause 4: Poor Quality**
- Fix: Improve message quality, make more relevant and personalized
