## PERFORMANCE MONITORING

### Key Metrics to Track Daily

**Awareness Metrics:**
- **Impressions:** How many people saw the ad
- **Reach:** Unique users who saw the ad
- **Frequency:** Average times each person saw it (keep under 3)

**Engagement Metrics:**
- **CTR (Click-Through Rate):** 1-2% is good for local events
- **Link Clicks:** Direct measure of interest
- **Cost Per Click (CPC):** Target under $0.50

**Conversion Metrics:**
- **RSVP Form Submissions:** Track via website analytics
- **Cost Per RSVP:** Divide total spend by RSVPs (target under $5)

### When to Adjust

**If CTR < 0.5%:** Creative isn't resonating
- Test new video/images
- Revise ad copy for more urgency

**If CPC > $1.00:** Audience too broad or competitive
- Narrow radius to 10 miles
- Refine age range (21-45)

**If Frequency > 3:** Audience fatigue
- Refresh creative
- Expand radius slightly
