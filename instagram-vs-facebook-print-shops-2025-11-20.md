# Instagram vs Facebook: B2B Print Shop Platform Comparison 2024-2025

## Executive Summary

**VERDICT: Facebook Primary (60%), Instagram Secondary (40%)**

For Print Junkie AZ targeting B2B clients in Phoenix, **Facebook should be the primary platform** with Instagram as a strong secondary. This is based on:

- B2B decision-makers spend **74% more time on Facebook** than other users
- **70% of B2B buyers** use Facebook for research and purchasing decisions
- **Facebook Groups** offer superior local community access and lead generation
- **Lower advertising costs** for manufacturing/print (CPM $2.40 vs $6.59 average)
- **Better messaging conversions** for quote requests (70% lower CPL via Messenger)
- Instagram offers **higher engagement** (0.50% vs 0.15%) but **lower purchase intent** for B2B services

**Recommended Strategy:**
- Facebook: 3x/week (Groups + Page), Facebook Ads primary channel
- Instagram: 2x/week (portfolio showcase, cross-post from Facebook)
- Total time investment: 8-10 hours/month (both platforms)

## 6. Organic Reach: Instagram vs Facebook for Local B2B Discovery

### Organic Reach Reality Check (2024-2025)

**The Bad News - Organic Reach is Dead:**
- **Facebook organic reach: 1.37%** (2024 average)
- **Facebook median engagement: 0.2%**
- Some studies cite Facebook as low as **0.05% organic reach**
- **Instagram organic reach: 3%** (only 3% of followers see content)
- Both platforms dropped **50%+ since 2015**

**The Good News - Algorithm Changes Favor Discovery:**
- **Up to 50% of Facebook feed** = content from accounts users DON'T follow
- **Follower count no longer determines reach** (quality > quantity)
- **Instagram Reels: 4x the reach** of traditional posts
- **Niche content more discoverable** via AI personalization

**Platform-Specific Organic Performance:**

**Facebook:**
- **Organic reach: 1.37%** (if you have 100 followers, 1-2 see your post)
- **Facebook Groups: 18% more engagement** than Pages (bypass algorithm)
- **Local targeting** still works better than Instagram for geo-discovery

**Instagram:**
- **Organic reach: 3%** (if you have 100 followers, 3 see your post)
- **Reels: 4x reach** of static posts (algorithm prioritizes video)
- **Engagement rate: 0.50%** vs Facebook's 0.15% (higher interaction per impression)

**LinkedIn (for comparison):**
- **Organic reach: 6.4%**
- **Engagement rate: 3%**
- **77% of B2B marketers** say LinkedIn is top platform

**For Print Junkie AZ - Organic Strategy:**

**Facebook Organic:**
1. **Facebook Groups = primary organic strategy** (bypass Page algorithm)
2. Post in **5-10 Phoenix business Groups** weekly (portfolio + tips)
3. **Cross-post** from Instagram to Page (2x/week minimum)
4. **Video content** prioritized by algorithm (Reels, production time-lapses)

**Instagram Organic:**
1. **Reels = 4x reach** - prioritize video content (behind-the-scenes, installs)
2. **Portfolio posts** for engagement, not reach (showcase quality work)
3. **Local hashtags** (#PhoenixSmallBusiness, #ArizonaPrint, #PhoenixEvents)
4. **Expect 3-5% reach** - focus on **converting engaged followers**, not viral growth

**Reality Check:**
With **100 followers**, your organic reach is:
- Facebook Page: **1-2 people/post** (useless without Groups)
- Instagram: **3-5 people/post** (better, but still limited)
- Facebook Groups: **50-100 people/post** (if you join 5 Groups with 1K+ members)

**THIS IS WHY FACEBOOK ADS ARE CRITICAL.** Organic reach is dead, but Facebook's low CPM ($2.40 for manufacturing) makes paid reach affordable.

**Sources:** Keefomatic Organic Reach 2025, Addictive Digital Organic Decline Study, Click Insights Algorithm Guide, Red Mallard Facebook B2B Report
