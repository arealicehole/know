## Instagram Strategy

### Format Performance Hierarchy (2024-2025 Data)

**1. Instagram Reels (Best for Reach)**
- **Engagement Rate:** 1.23% (beats photos at 0.70%, carousels at 0.99%)
- **Reach Advantage:** 2x higher reach rate vs other formats
- **Algorithm Priority:** Instagram actively pushes Reels beyond existing followers
- **Best For:** Small-to-mid accounts (<50K followers)
- **Optimal Length:** 20-25 seconds for maximum completion rates
- **Engagement Peak:** 15-30 seconds (sweet spot)

**2. Carousels (Best for Saves)**
- **Engagement Rate:** 0.99% (0.55% in some 2025 studies)
- **Save Performance:** 12% more likely to be saved vs Reels
- **Best For:** Large accounts (>50K followers) and portfolio showcases
- **Advantage:** Reference content, detailed product displays
- **Note:** YoY decrease of 15% in engagement (still valuable for specific use cases)

**3. Static Posts (Lowest Performance)**
- **Engagement Rate:** 0.70% (0.45% in 2025 data)
- **Reach:** 13.14% (vs Reels at 14.45%+)
- **Use Sparingly:** Only for high-quality portfolio pieces

### Content Mix Recommendation for Print Shops

**60% Reels** (Reach & Discovery)
- Production time-lapses (20-30 sec)
- Behind-the-scenes process videos
- Before/after installations
- Quick design tips

**30% Carousels** (Portfolio & Education)
- Multi-image project showcases
- Educational design guides
- Client testimonials with visuals
- Product variety displays

**10% Static Posts** (Premium Portfolio)
- Hero shots of best work
- Major project announcements
- Milestone celebrations

### Instagram Posting Strategy

**Frequency:** 3-5 times per week
- **Monday:** Reel (motivational/inspirational project)
- **Wednesday:** Carousel (educational or portfolio)
- **Friday:** Reel (BTS or process video)
- **Optional:** 2 additional posts (mix formats)

**Best Times:** Post 5-10 minutes before your audience's peak activity (allows followers to engage first, boosting algorithm signals)

## TikTok Strategy

### Why TikTok Matters for B2B Print Shops

**Game-Changing Data:**
- Belgian B2B startup: **14x more signups** using TikTok educational content
- Zendesk (B2B): 1.16% avg engagement, 185K avg views
- Biteable: 100M+ organic views with personality-driven content
- **32.9% of TikTok users are 35+** (B2B audience growing rapidly)

**TikTok Evolution:** No longer just Gen Z entertainment. Now a **search & discovery engine** with 40% increase in searches vs 2024.

### TikTok Algorithm Mastery (2024-2025)

**Primary Ranking Signals (In Order):**

1. **Watch Time = King** (most influential factor)
   - Granular checkpoints tracked: 3s, 15s, 30s, 60s
   - Replays weighted MORE than likes/comments
   - Session depth: How long users stay after watching

2. **Engagement Signals**
   - Comments > Shares > Likes (in order of importance)
   - Saves increasingly important

3. **Video Completion Rate**
   - Higher weight in 2025 vs 2024
   - 30% of short videos achieve 81%+ completion rate

4. **Search Optimization**
   - Query match emphasis (videos answering specific search intent)
   - Keyword-rich captions critical
   - Hashtag relevance

### TikTok Content Formula for Print Shops

**The Make-or-Break Hook (First 1.3 Seconds)**

**Hook increases retention by 65%** if done right. Three proven hook categories:

**1. Pattern Interruption**
- "Stop scrolling if you've ever wondered how business cards are made..."
- "POV: You're watching $500 worth of printing in 30 seconds..."
- "They said print is dead. Watch this..." [shows busy print shop]

**2. Visual Impact**
- Start with unexpected visual (close-up of printer in action)
- Bold color reveal (blank white ‚Üí vibrant full-color print)
- Fast-motion production shot

**3. Surprising Statement/Question**
- "We printed 10,000 business cards today. Here's what most people don't know..."
- "This small business card order costs $75. Our profit margin will shock you..."
- "3 design mistakes that make your print look cheap..."

### Optimal Video Length (2024-2025 Data)

**TikTok Sweet Spots:**
- **21-34 seconds:** Highest engagement rate (2025 data)
- **30-60 seconds:** Majority of viral content in 2025
- **45 seconds:** Recommended balance of depth + pace

**Comparison:**
- TikTok engagement rate: **2.34%** (overall average)
- Instagram Reels: 1.48%
- YouTube Shorts: 0.91%

**For accounts with 100K-500K followers:**
- TikTok: **9.74%** engagement
- Instagram Reels: 6.59%

**Winner:** TikTok for engagement, especially for growing accounts

## Facebook Strategy

### The Dual Strategy: Pages + Groups

**Key Finding:** Facebook Groups see **18% more comments** and **10% more shares** vs Business Pages. Groups bypass algorithm restrictions for organic reach.

**However:** You need BOTH. Here's why:

### Facebook Business Page (Foundation)

**Purpose:** Brand awareness, advertising, credibility

**Advantages:**
- Access to Facebook Ads Manager (targeting + analytics)
- Indexed by search engines (SEO value)
- Professional presence for legitimacy
- Messenger integration for customer service

**Posting Strategy:**
- **Frequency:** 3-5 times per week
- **Content:** Portfolio showcases, educational posts, promotions
- **Cross-post from Instagram:** Same content, adapted captions

### Facebook Groups (Relationship Building)

**Purpose:** Word-of-mouth marketing, community engagement, local referrals

**Strategy:**
1. **Join 5-10 Local Business Groups:**
   - "Phoenix Small Business Owners"
   - "Arizona Entrepreneurs"
   - "[City Name] Buy/Sell/Trade"
   - Local chamber of commerce groups
   - Industry-specific groups (realtors, event planners)

2. **Engagement Rules:**
   - Participate DAILY (not just self-promote)
   - Answer questions authentically
   - Share valuable insights (no sales pitch)
   - Offer help before asking for business
   - Share success stories (with permission)

3. **Referral Strategy:**
   - Groups are "digital word-of-mouth marketing"
   - Must have Business Page to show up in discussions
   - Soft-sell approach: "We do that! DM me for details"
   - Ask satisfied clients to mention you in groups

### Facebook Content Performance (2024)

**Best Performing Content Types:**

1. **Client Success Stories** (tagged businesses = amplification)
2. **Educational Carousels** (design tips, material guides)
3. **Video Content** (production BTS, time-lapses)
4. **Local Community Posts** (supporting other businesses)
5. **Promotional Offers** (limited-time discounts)

**Content Mix:**
- 50% Educational/Value-add
- 30% Portfolio/Showcase
- 20% Promotional

### Facebook Best Practices for Print Shops

**Page Optimization:**
- Complete business information (hours, location, phone, website)
- Professional cover photo (portfolio piece or team photo)
- Pin important post (current promotion or best portfolio piece)
- Enable reviews and respond to ALL of them
- Use Facebook Shops if selling products

**Posting Strategy:**
- **Best Times:** Weekday mornings (8-10am) and lunch (12-1pm)
- **Frequency:** 3-5x per week (daily for growth mode)
- **Cross-posting:** Repost Instagram content (save time)
- **Stories:** Daily (quick updates, polls, BTS)

**Group Participation:**
- Daily engagement (5-10 min)
- Help-first mindset
- Build reputation as expert
- Soft-sell only when relevant

- Sticking to a schedule for long period > sporadic bursts

**Manufacturing/Print Business Standard:** **3-4 posts per week per platform**
